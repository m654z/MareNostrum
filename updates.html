MARE NOSTRUM Dev Diaries

Update #1: Announced!

After ~6 months I've finally announced Mare Nostrum! Go check out the announcement post on Reddit.

Currently I'm working on the closed Alpha version, which will feature most mechanics, although in a simplified form (internal politics, war, etc.)

After that, I'll probably focus on the AI (which works, but currently ends up in a stalemate with the other AI players in maybe 20 turns) and diplomacy (there is only one working diplomatic action, Declare War).
